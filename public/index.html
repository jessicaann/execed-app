Executive Education - made easy.

With Exec-Ed App, creating and delivering the materials necessary for a program is easy. And even better, administrators can provide participants with access to a beautiful, easy to use portal to access those materials.

Administrators

In Executive Education App, you can create, edit, and delete all of the components of a successful program schedule and give participants access to their schedule and relevant prework materials all in one easy-to-access place.

View details »

As an Admin, you can...

  • 1. Create your teammates, instructors, and prework as necessary.
  • 2. Create a new schedule.
  • 3. Add new sessions to that schedule.
  • 4. Create your users, assign them schedules and marvel at their success!

Participants
